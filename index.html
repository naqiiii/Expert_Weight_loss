Progress review and medication

Expert Care provided at your local
pharmacy

Have your vitals checked and collect your treatment from a local participating
pharmacy at a time that suits you. Expert care delivered by trusted local
community pharmacies.

You will have a 1-1 training session with the pharmacist who will show you how to use your new treatment.
Track your progress through our BMI check. The pharmacy team will check your weight and height to calculate your BMI.
You will also be offered to have your Blood Pressure, Pulse and Waist Circumference measured.
During your pharmacy review, you will have a chance to review progress and discuss any side effects with the pharmacist.
The vitals measurements are entered into the system which you can access through your dedicated dashboard to track your progress.
You will have access to our Health Hub which provides useful information about your treatment and weight loss in general.
